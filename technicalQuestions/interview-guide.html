📋 通用问题模板 / General Question Templates

🏗️ 项目经验 / Project Experience

ASK:

"I see you worked on [Project X] at [Company]. Can you walk me through the architecture and your specific contributions?"

Follow-up questions:

  • "What was the most challenging technical problem you faced on this project?"
  • "How did you handle [specific technology/pattern mentioned in resume]?"
  • "What would you do differently if you were to rebuild it from scratch?"
  • "How did you measure the success of this project?"

🚨 卡壳应对 / If Candidate Gets Stuck

😶 回答太泛，缺乏细节 / Answer too vague, lacks detail
提示等级Script
Light"Can you be more specific? For example, what APIs or libraries did you use?"
Medium"Walk me through a specific technical decision you made — what options did you consider?"
👥 只谈团队成果，不谈个人 / Only talks about team, not self
"That sounds like a great team effort. What was YOUR specific contribution?"
😰 紧张不自信 / Nervous, not confident
"Take your time. There's no wrong answer — I just want to understand how you think about these problems."
🚩 简历上写的但答不上来 / Can't explain something on their resume
🚩 Red Flag：不要当场点破，记录下来。注意观察是完全不了解还是只是表述困难。
Red flag: Note it down, don't call it out. Observe whether they truly don't know or just struggle to articulate.

🔍 技术栈深挖 / Tech Stack Deep-Dive

Based on the candidate's resume, pick the most relevant area and go deep:

🖥️ Frontend (if applicable)
  • Component design patterns and state management (Redux? Context? Zustand?)
  • Performance optimization (lazy loading, memoization, virtualization)
  • Accessibility and responsive design practices
  • Build toolchain and bundling (Webpack, Vite, etc.)

Sample deep-dive questions:

"You mention using React — can you explain how you structured state management? When would you choose Context API over Redux?"
"Tell me about a performance issue you identified and resolved in a frontend app."
⚙️ Backend (if applicable)
  • API design — REST vs GraphQL trade-offs
  • Database design and query optimization
  • Scalability patterns (caching, load balancing, message queues)
  • Authentication and security practices

Sample deep-dive questions:

"How did you handle database performance at scale? What indexing strategies did you use?"
"Walk me through how you designed the API for [project]. Why did you choose REST/GraphQL?"
🔧 General Engineering
  • Testing strategies: unit, integration, E2E — coverage goals
  • CI/CD pipeline design and deployment strategy
  • Code review practices and mentoring experience
  • How they approach and prioritize technical debt

Sample deep-dive questions:

"What's your testing philosophy? How do you decide what to test and at what level?"
"How do you balance shipping features quickly versus paying down tech debt?"

💡 Tips for Interviewer

  1. 跟着简历走 / Follow the resume：提前标记 2-3 个感兴趣的项目或技术点。Pre-mark 2-3 interesting projects or tech points before the interview.
  2. 深而不广 / Go deep, not wide：选一个方向深入比什么都问一遍好。Better to go deep on one topic than skim many.
  3. 听思考过程 / Listen for thought process：关注候选人怎么分析问题，而不仅仅是答案。Focus on how they analyze problems, not just answers.
  4. 注意红旗 / Watch for red flags：简历上写的但答不上来的内容。Things on the resume they can't explain.
  5. 时间控制 / Time management：不要在一个问题上花超过 5 分钟。Don't spend more than 5 min on any single question.
