# 💬 Section 2 — Resume-based Technical Q&A (~10 min)

## 目的 / Purpose

根据候选人的简历，深入询问技术问题，评估其技术深度、实际经验和问题解决能力。

Based on the candidate's resume, ask in-depth technical questions to assess their technical depth, hands-on experience, and problem-solving ability.

---

## ⏱️ Flow

| Step | Content | Time |
|------|---------|------|
| 1 | 针对简历项目提问 / Questions on resume projects | ~4 min |
| 2 | 技术深挖 / Technical deep-dive | ~4 min |
| 3 | 情景/设计问题 / Scenario/design questions | ~2 min |

---

## 通用问题模板 / General Question Templates

### 项目经验 / Project Experience

**ASK:**
> *"I see you worked on [Project X] at [Company]. Can you walk me through the architecture and your specific contributions?"*

**Follow-up questions:**
- *"What was the most challenging technical problem you faced on this project?"*
- *"How did you handle [specific technology/pattern mentioned in resume]?"*
- *"What would you do differently if you were to rebuild it from scratch?"*

---

### 技术栈深挖 / Tech Stack Deep-Dive

**Based on resume, pick relevant areas:**

#### Frontend (if applicable)
- Component design patterns and state management
- Performance optimization strategies
- Accessibility and responsive design

#### Backend (if applicable)
- API design and system architecture
- Database design and optimization
- Scalability and reliability

#### General
- Testing strategies and CI/CD
- Code review practices
- How they handle technical debt

## 💡 Tips for Interviewer

1. **跟着简历走 / Follow the resume:** 提前标记 2-3 个感兴趣的项目或技术点。
2. **深而不广 / Go deep, not wide:** 选一个方向深入比什么都问一遍好。
3. **听思考过程 / Listen for thought process:** 关注候选人怎么分析问题，而不仅仅是答案。
4. **注意红旗 / Watch for red flags:** 简历上写的但答不上来的内容。
