🗺️ 面试流程总览 / Interview Flow Overview

#环节 / Section时间 / Time要点 / Key Points
① 自我介绍 / Self Introduction ~5 min 面试官开场 → 候选人介绍背景
② 技术问答 / Technical Q&A ~10 min 基于简历深挖技术细节
③ 编程测试 / Coding Test ~10 min 单调递增函数反函数 · 二分查找
④ 候选人提问 / Candidate Q&A ~5 min 候选人提问 → 结束面试

🎙️ 开场台词 / Opening Script

English

"Today we have about 30 minutes, and it'll be four parts: first a quick self-introduction, then some questions based on your resume, followed by a short coding exercise, and finally some time for you to ask me anything. Sound good? Let's jump in."

中文

"今天一共大概 30 分钟，分四个部分：先请你做个简短的自我介绍，接着我根据简历问几个技术问题，然后一起做道编程题，最后留时间给你提问。我们开始吧。"

① 🙋 自我介绍 / Self Introduction ~5 min

🎤 引导候选人自我介绍 / Ask for Self-Introduction

"Alright, let's get started. Could you give me a brief introduction about yourself — maybe 1 to 2 minutes?"
"好的，那我们开始吧。能请你做一个简短的自我介绍吗？大概 1 到 2 分钟就好。"
~5 min

🙋 进入 Section 1 详细指南 →

② 💬 技术问答 / Resume-based Technical Q&A ~10 min

🎤 引导进入技术问答 / Introduce Technical Q&A

"Great, thanks for sharing that. Now I'd like to ask you a few questions based on your resume — just to learn more about some of the projects and technologies you've worked with."
"很好，谢谢你的分享。接下来我想根据你的简历问几个问题——主要是想多了解一下你做过的一些项目和用过的技术。"
~10 min

💬 进入 Section 2 详细指南 →

③ 💻 编程测试 / Coding Test ~10 min

🎤 引导进入编程测试 / Introduce Coding Exercise

"Alright, let's move on to a short coding exercise. I'll describe a problem, and we can work through it together."
"好的，接下来我们做一道编程题。我会描述一个问题，我们可以一起讨论。"
~10 min

💻 进入 Section 3 详细指南 →

④ ❓ 候选人提问 / Candidate Q&A ~5 min

🎤 引导候选人提问 / Invite Candidate Questions

"Great, that wraps up my questions. Do you have any questions for us?"
"好的，我的问题已经问完了。你有什么问题想问我们吗？"
~5 min

❓ 进入 Section 4 详细指南 →

👋 结束台词 / Closing Script

English

"Thank you so much for your time today, Jorge. Hope you have a great rest of your day!"

中文

"非常感谢你今天的时间，Jorge。祝你今天一切顺利！"
