🗺️ 面试流程总览 / Interview Flow Overview

#环节 / Section时间 / Time要点 / Key Points
① 自我介绍 / Self Introduction ~5 min 面试官开场 → 候选人介绍背景
② 技术问答 / Technical Q&A ~10 min 基于简历深挖技术细节
③ 编程测试 / Coding Test ~10 min 单调递增函数反函数 · 二分查找
④ 候选人提问 / Candidate Q&A ~5 min 候选人提问 → 结束面试

🎙️ 开场台词 / Opening Script

English

"Hi Jorge, thanks for taking the time to speak with us today. My name is [Your Name], I'm a [Your Title] on the [Team Name] team at Microsoft. We're working on [brief team description]."
"This will be about a 30-minute phone screen. Here's how we'll spend our time: first, I'd love to hear a quick introduction about your background — just a couple of minutes. Then we'll chat about some of the technical work on your resume. After that, we'll do a short coding exercise together, and finally you'll have a chance to ask me any questions. Does that sound good?"

中文

"你好 Jorge，感谢你今天抽时间和我们交流。我是 [你的名字]，在 Microsoft [团队名称] 团队担任 [你的职位]。我们团队主要负责 [团队工作简述]。"
"今天的电话面试大约 30 分钟。流程是这样的：首先请你做一个简短的自我介绍，大约两分钟；然后我会根据你的简历聊一些技术问题；接着我们一起做一道编程题；最后留时间给你向我提问。可以开始吗？"

① 🙋 自我介绍 / Self Introduction ~5 min

🎤 引导候选人自我介绍 / Ask for Self-Introduction

"Alright, let's get started. Could you give me a brief introduction about yourself — maybe 1 to 2 minutes? I'd love to hear about your background, what you've been working on recently, and what drew you to this role."
"好的，那我们开始吧。能请你做一个简短的自我介绍吗？大概 1 到 2 分钟就好。可以聊聊你的背景、最近在做什么、以及是什么吸引你来申请这个职位。"
~5 min

🙋 进入 Section 1 详细指南 →

🔗 衔接 ①→② / Transition: Self-Intro → Technical Q&A

①→②
"Great, thanks for that overview Jorge. I'd love to dig a bit deeper into some of the technical work you mentioned — especially around [pick a topic from their intro, e.g. 'the Vue-to-React migration at Emeris']. Let me ask you a few questions based on your resume."
"很好，谢谢你的介绍 Jorge。我想更深入了解你提到的一些技术工作——特别是 [根据候选人介绍选一个话题，如 'Emeris 的 Vue→React 迁移']。接下来我根据你的简历问几个问题。"
💡 衔接技巧 / Transition Tips
技巧：过渡时尽量引用候选人刚才提到的内容，让对话自然衔接而非生硬切换。例如候选人提到了 design system，可以说 "You mentioned building a design system — I'd love to hear more about that architecture…"

Tip: Reference something the candidate just said to make the transition feel natural. This shows you're actively listening and builds rapport.

② 💬 技术问答 / Resume-based Technical Q&A ~10 min

🎤 引导进入技术问答 / Introduce Technical Q&A

"Great, thanks for sharing that. Now I'd like to ask you a few questions based on your resume — just to learn more about some of the projects and technologies you've worked with."
"很好，谢谢你的分享。接下来我想根据你的简历问几个问题——主要是想多了解一下你做过的一些项目和用过的技术。"
~10 min

💬 进入 Section 2 详细指南 →

🔗 衔接 ②→③ / Transition: Technical Q&A → Coding

②→③
"Awesome, those are really helpful insights into your experience. Now I'd like to switch gears a bit — let's do a short coding exercise together. I'll share a problem with you, and we can work through it. It's totally fine to think out loud — I'm more interested in your thought process than getting a perfect solution right away. Let me share my screen…"
"非常好，这些对了解你的经验很有帮助。现在我们换个节奏——接下来做一道编程题。我会把题目分享给你，我们一起来做。你可以边想边说，我更关心你的思考过程，不需要一开始就写出完美代码。让我共享屏幕……"
💡 衔接技巧 / Transition Tips
技巧：切换到 coding 前先确认候选人准备好了。给一句 "Take a moment to get comfortable" 的缓冲。如果候选人技术问答表现出色，可以说 "I'm impressed by your depth — let's see how you approach a coding problem" 来保持积极气氛。

Tip: Before switching to coding, confirm the candidate is ready. A brief pause helps reduce anxiety. If the candidate did well in the technical Q&A, acknowledge it to maintain positive momentum.

③ 💻 编程测试 / Coding Test ~10 min

~10 min

💻 进入 Section 3 详细指南 →

🔗 衔接 ③→④ / Transition: Coding → Candidate Q&A

③→④
"Great work on that problem, Jorge. Let's wrap up the coding part here. We have about 5 minutes left — this is your time now. Do you have any questions for me about the team, the role, or anything else about working at Microsoft?"
"这道题做得不错，Jorge。编程部分到此结束。我们还有大约 5 分钟时间——现在是你的时间了。你对我们的团队、这个职位、或者在 Microsoft 工作有什么想问的吗？"
💡 衔接技巧 / Transition Tips
技巧：不论候选人 coding 表现如何，都要给予正面的过渡。如果表现不佳，说 "Thanks for working through that with me" 而非评论结果。让候选人在提问环节感到被尊重。

Tip: Regardless of coding performance, transition positively. If they struggled, say "Thanks for working through that with me" rather than commenting on the outcome.
⏱️ 时间不够时 / Running low on time：
"We're running a bit short on time, but I want to make sure you get to ask at least one question. What's the most important thing you'd like to know?"
"时间有点紧张了，但我想确保你至少能问一个问题。你最想了解什么？"

④ ❓ 候选人提问 / Candidate Q&A ~5 min

~5 min

👋 结束台词 / Closing Script

English — Standard Closing

"Thank you so much for your time today, Jorge. It was really great speaking with you and learning about your experience. From here, our recruiting team will be in touch with next steps — usually within [timeframe, e.g. a week or so]. If you think of any other questions in the meantime, feel free to reach out to your recruiter."
"I hope you have a great rest of your day. Thanks again, and take care!"

中文 — 标准结束语

"非常感谢你今天的时间，Jorge。和你交流非常愉快，也了解了你很多出色的经验。接下来我们的招聘团队会联系你告知下一步安排——通常在 [时间范围，如一周左右] 内。如果你后续想到什么问题，随时联系你的 recruiter。"
"祝你今天一切顺利。再次感谢，再见！"
🎭 不同场景的结束语变体 / Closing Variants by Scenario

✅ 候选人表现优秀 / Strong Candidate

"I really enjoyed our conversation today — you clearly have a strong grasp of frontend architecture and system design. I'll make sure to pass along my positive feedback. Looking forward to hopefully working together!"
"今天的交流非常愉快——你对前端架构的理解确实很深入。我会把积极的反馈传达给团队。希望未来有机会一起合作！"

⚖️ 候选人表现一般 / Neutral

"Thanks for taking the time to chat today, Jorge. It was great getting to know you better. Our team will review everything and get back to you through the recruiting team."
"感谢你今天抽时间来聊，Jorge。很高兴能更多地了解你。我们团队会综合评估，然后通过招聘团队联系你。"

⏱️ 时间超了 / Over Time

"I realize we're running a bit over time — I apologize for that. Thank you for your patience. I'll let you go, and our recruiter will follow up on next steps. Great chatting with you!"
"抱歉我们超时了一点。感谢你的耐心。我就不耽误你了，recruiter 会跟进后续安排。和你聊天很开心！"
