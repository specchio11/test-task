# ❓ Section 4 — Candidate Q&A (~5 min)

## 目的 / Purpose

给候选人机会提问，了解团队、项目和职位。同时也是评估候选人对职位兴趣程度和思考深度的好机会。

Give the candidate a chance to ask questions about the team, project, and role. This is also a good opportunity to assess the candidate's interest level and depth of thinking.

---

## ⏱️ Flow

| Step | Content | Time |
|------|---------|------|
| 1 | 邀请候选人提问 / Invite candidate questions | ~0.5 min |
| 2 | 回答候选人问题 / Answer questions | ~3.5 min |
| 3 | 结束面试 / Wrap up | ~1 min |

---

## 过渡话术 / Transition Script

**SAY:**
> *"Great, that wraps up the technical portion. Now it's your turn — do you have any questions for me about the team, the role, or anything else?"*

**中文版:**
> *"好的，技术部分就到这里了。现在轮到你了——你有什么想问我的吗？关于团队、职位或者其他任何方面都可以。"*

---

## 候选人常见问题及建议回答 / Common Candidate Questions & Suggested Answers

### 关于团队 / About the Team
| Question | Suggested Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| "How big is the team?" | _(根据实际情况回答)_ |
| "What does a typical day look like?" | _(描述日常工作节奏)_ |
| "How is the team structured?" | _(介绍团队结构)_ |

### 关于技术 / About Technology
| Question | Suggested Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| "What tech stack do you use?" | _(介绍主要技术栈)_ |
| "How do you handle code reviews?" | _(描述代码审查流程)_ |
| "What's the testing strategy?" | _(介绍测试策略)_ |

### 关于成长 / About Growth
| Question | Suggested Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| "What does career growth look like?" | _(描述晋升路径)_ |
| "How do you support learning?" | _(学习资源和机会)_ |

---

## 结束话术 / Closing Script

**SAY:**
> *"Thank you so much for your time today, [Candidate Name]. It was great speaking with you. We'll be in touch about next steps within [timeframe]. If you think of any other questions in the meantime, feel free to reach out to your recruiter."*

**中文版:**
> *"非常感谢你今天的时间，[候选人姓名]。和你交流很愉快。我们会在 [时间范围] 内联系你告知下一步安排。如果你之后还有其他问题，随时可以联系你的招聘人员。"*

---
