❓ 候选人提问 / Candidate Q&A ~5 min

⏱️ Flow

#ContentTime
1邀请候选人提问 / Invite candidate questions~0.5 min
2回答候选人问题 / Answer questions~3.5 min
3结束面试 / Wrap up~1 min

🎙️ 过渡话术 / Transition Script

🇺🇸 English

"Great, that wraps up the technical portion. Now it's your turn — do you have any questions for me about the team, the role, or anything else?"

🇨🇳 中文版

"好的，技术部分就到这里了。现在轮到你了——你有什么想问我的吗？关于团队、职位或者其他任何方面都可以。"

💬 候选人常见问题及建议回答 / Common Questions & Suggested Answers

🏢 关于团队 / About the Team
Candidate Might AskSuggested Response Outline
"How big is the team?"(介绍团队规模、sub-team 结构)
"What does a typical day/sprint look like?"(描述日常工作节奏、stand-up、sprint cadence)
"How is the team structured?"(PM / Design / Dev 比例，跨组协作方式)
"What's the on-call situation?"(on-call rotation 频率和负担)
🛠️ 关于技术 / About Technology
Candidate Might AskSuggested Response Outline
"What tech stack do you use?"(前端框架、后端语言、云服务、CI/CD)
"How do you handle code reviews?"(PR 流程、review 标准、turnaround time)
"What's the testing strategy?"(unit / integration / E2E 覆盖率目标)
"How do you handle tech debt?"(tech debt sprint、20% time、prioritization)
📈 关于成长 / About Growth
Candidate Might AskSuggested Response Outline
"What does career growth look like?"(IC track vs manager track、晋升周期)
"How do you support learning & development?"(learning budget、conferences、内部培训)
"What's the biggest challenge the team is facing?"(诚实回答当前挑战，展现团队透明度)
面试官注意 / Interviewer Note：候选人提问的质量也是一种信号。提出有深度、经过思考的问题（如关于技术栈的 trade-off、团队协作模式）是加分项。只问薪资福利不一定是减分项，但如果完全没有问题，可能说明对职位兴趣不高。

Interviewer Note: The quality of a candidate's questions is also a signal. Thoughtful, well-considered questions (about tech trade-offs, team collaboration) are a plus. Only asking about comp/benefits isn't necessarily bad, but having no questions at all might indicate low interest.

👋 结束话术 / Closing Script

🇺🇸 English

"Thank you so much for your time today, [Candidate Name]. It was great speaking with you. We'll be in touch about next steps within [timeframe]. If you think of any other questions in the meantime, feel free to reach out to your recruiter."

🇨🇳 中文版

"非常感谢你今天的时间，[候选人姓名]。和你交流很愉快。我们会在 [时间范围] 内联系你告知下一步安排。如果你之后还有其他问题，随时可以联系你的招聘人员。"

📝 Evaluation

Observation Checklist

  • 候选人提出了有深度的问题 / Candidate asked insightful questions
  • 候选人表现出对职位的真诚兴趣 / Showed genuine interest in the role
  • 候选人的问题反映了对团队和工作的思考 / Questions reflected thoughtfulness about team and work
  • 候选人礼貌专业地结束了面试 / Ended the interview politely and professionally
