# 🙋 Section 1 — Self Introduction (~5 min)

## 目的 / Purpose

面试官进行自我介绍并简要介绍团队和职位，然后让候选人做一个简短的自我介绍。

The interviewer introduces themselves and gives a brief overview of the team and role, then asks the candidate for a short self-introduction.

---

## ⏱️ Flow

| Step | Content | Time |
|------|---------|------|
| 1 | 面试官自我介绍 / Interviewer introduction | ~1 min |
| 2 | 简要介绍团队和角色 / Brief team & role overview | ~1 min |
| 3 | 候选人自我介绍 / Candidate elevator pitch | ~2 min |
| 4 | 暖场过渡 / Warm-up transition | ~1 min |

---

## 面试官开场白 / Interviewer Opening Script

**SAY:**
> *"Hi [Candidate Name], thanks for taking the time to speak with us today. My name is [Your Name], I'm a [Your Title] on the [Team Name] team at Microsoft. We're working on [brief description of what the team does]."*
>
> *"This will be a 30-minute phone screen. We'll start with introductions, then I'll ask a few technical questions based on your resume, followed by a short coding exercise, and finally you'll have a chance to ask me any questions. Sound good?"*

**中文版:**
> *"你好 [候选人姓名]，感谢你今天抽时间和我们交流。我是 [你的名字]，在 Microsoft [团队名称] 团队担任 [你的职位]。我们团队主要负责 [团队工作简述]。"*
>
> *"今天的电话面试大约 30 分钟。我们先互相介绍一下，然后我会根据你的简历问几个技术问题，接着是一道编程题，最后你可以问我任何问题。可以吗？"*

---

## 候选人自我介绍 / Candidate Self-Introduction

**ASK:**
> *"Great, could you give me a quick 1-2 minute introduction about yourself — your background, what you've been working on recently, and what interests you about this role?"*

**📝 Evaluation Points**

- [ ] 清晰简洁地介绍了自己的背景 / Clear and concise background summary
- [ ] 提到了相关的技术栈和经验 / Mentioned relevant tech stack and experience
- [ ] 表达了对职位的兴趣和动机 / Expressed interest and motivation for the role
- [ ] 沟通流畅自信 / Fluent and confident communication

**💡 If candidate is too brief:**
> *"Could you tell me more about your most recent project?"*

**💡 If candidate is too verbose:**
> *"That's great context, thank you. Let's dive into some technical questions."*
